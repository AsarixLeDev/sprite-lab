# Troubleshooting

## No usable images found

Choose another folder containing PNG, JPEG, or WebP images. Damaged files stay out; your originals are not changed.

## Source or license is missing

Add where the images came from and the permission that allows their use. Do not continue until both are known.

## Image descriptions are not set up

Choose a local or hosted description option, or continue with manual descriptions.

## Sign-in needs attention

Open connection settings, sign in again, then choose **Test connection**. Sign-in details are not saved in the project.

## Training is temporarily unavailable

Choose **Show what to fix** and complete the listed repair. Your dataset and completed work remain safe.

## A run was interrupted

Choose **Check saved work**. Continue only when Sprite Lab shows **Ready to continue**.

## Connection lost

Check your internet connection, then choose **Try connection again**. No new remote work starts automatically.

## Sprite Lab ran into a problem

Choose **Try again**. If the problem returns, create a support report. The normal error page does not show a raw error trace or reveal sign-in details.
