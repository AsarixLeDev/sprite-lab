# Local and hosted compute

Sprite Lab can run work on your computer or through a hosted service when those options are installed.

## Local

- Images and work stay on your computer.
- There is no hosted-service charge.
- Training speed depends on your computer.
- Closing the program can interrupt active work, but completed work is preserved.

## Hosted

- A remote service performs the work.
- Images needed for the operation may be sent to that service.
- The service may charge money.
- Sprite Lab shows the service, spending limit, and estimated cost before one final confirmation.

A lost connection never starts new remote work automatically. Check the connection and choose **Try connection again**.

Choose the local option when privacy and no service charge matter most. Choose hosted work only after you understand the service's privacy terms and cost.
